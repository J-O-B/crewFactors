CrewFactors - Training Analytics

On uploading your recordings, you will receive a thorough report that shows how efficient communication has been between pilots.

Although the science behind CrewFactors is complex, our feedback that will be aviailable to you is easy to understand and beneficial for both students and instructors.

  • Communication Score
    • This data will provide you with an overall communications score providing a simplified total score to gather a quick overview of communications not of just one crewmember, but as a team score.
  • Accommodation Level
    • Accommodation relates to how in sync team members were with one another. This phenomenon has a direct correlation to how well crewmembers work as a team.
  • Speaker Ratio
    • Speaker ratio metrics will display the overall quantity of speech compared to one another. We know through CRM studies that the target in flight crew is approximately 49% Captain, 50% First Officer.
  • Overlap
    • Overlap refers to periods where crew members were speaking at the same time. As successful communication is about both speaking, and listening.
